About Us


Following the dramatic win in 2021 by Maneskin with their Rock Classic "Zitti e Buoni", the Eurovision Song Contest is back for 2022 in the Northern Italian city of Turin. Whilst last years competition was somewhat hampered by the Covid-19 crisis, this year marks a year where Europe is embroiled in war. Ukraine are expected to perform well in the song contest as a show of unity from nations across Europe however nothing is certain until all the votes are tallied and verified. Many countries look set to do well in both the jury votes and the public televote with a plethora of quality acts from across Europe competing for the number 1 spot and the chance to host the Eurovision Song Contest in 2023. Songalong invites you to check out the entries, get familiar with your favourite acts in our gallery and to start the conversation about what will be an epic Song Contest.
